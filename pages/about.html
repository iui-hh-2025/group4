Why We Chose This Topic

We believe that artificial intelligence is one of the most powerful forces shaping our future — and that its environmental impact deserves just as much scrutiny as its technical potential. Our group was driven by a shared concern for climate resilience, ethical innovation, and the balance between progress and preservation.
